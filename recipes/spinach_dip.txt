Spinach Artichoke Dip

2-3 garlic cloves, smashed and chopped
1 (10 oz.) pkg. of fresh and boiled chopped spinach (thaw and drain well)
4 artichokes; boiled underwater for 25-45 minutes; only use hearts, chopped and drained
1 cup Mozzarella cheese
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Medium white sauce (2 tbsp. butter, 2 tbsp. flour, 1 cup milk)
Dash of hot sauce
